# Activity

Activity Indicator

Experiment to communicate activity in meetings, using only audio

Audio input, an avatar image, & dynamic motion to convey activity. The avatar “mosaic” increases/decreases in resolution & legibility depending on volume & frequency of input from the participant.

Example of active participation

**Technology:** P5.js
