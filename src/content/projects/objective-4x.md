# Objective 4x

objective 4x

A purpose built panning rig to film everyday objects through a microscope.

To create a panning rig that was slow & smooth enough to keep the camera in focus, stepper motors with 1000:1 gear reductions were used & an Arduino was added to control the direction & speed of the motion.
