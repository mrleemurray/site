# Social Radio

SOCIAL RADIO

Prototyping a speaker THAT TUNES INTO OTHER PEOPLES PLAYLISTS.

A prototype speaker that interfaces with a web app which collects, arranges & continually loops users’ playlists like radio stations. The speaker can scrub through the ‘stations‘ & play audio wirelessly.

**Technology:** web technologies

**Manufacturing:** Assembled from existing hardware, CAD created in SolidWorks, enclosure is 3D printed
