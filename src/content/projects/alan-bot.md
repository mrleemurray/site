# Alan Bot

alan bot

A chatbot platform for IOT interactions.

Through experimenting with chatbots, in particular Google Hangouts, we discovered that it could be harnessed as a platform agnostic & human friendly method of interfacing with the internet of things, including custom hardware.

**Technology:** Raspberry Pi, Python
