# Spring Table

spring table

A pop-up restaurant & app to promote the Nokia Lumia 800.

Nokia partnered with renowned designer Tom Dixon to create a pop-up restaurant for his

MOST exhibition

at the Milan Furniture Fair. Our team created an interactive menu, which guests could browse the daily food & drink selection, leave comments, reviews & photos of both their meal & the Nokia Lumia 800.

Stevie Parle

set up the pop-up restaurant

Spring Table

located at the grand 18th cloisters of the Science Museum. Parle is Head Chef and runs the critically acclaimed

Dock Kitchen

, a part of

Portobello Dock

and the home of

Tom Dixon

, London.
